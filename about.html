About

I was born on December 12, 1992, in Cali, Colombia, and have spent my career developing and honing my skills in a range of software development positions. My passion for game development and my expertise in languages such as C++ and C# have equipped me to work on exciting and innovative projects in the gaming industry.

My journey started as a Front End Developer at EXISTA YA, where I developed my expertise in web development, crafting dynamic and visually appealing websites. I then transitioned to game development roles at TNT3 Studio and Digital Virgo where I developed several mobile and web games.

At ParqueSoft, as a Lead Developer, I took my passion for game development further by working on a project which utilized the Unity Engine and AI. Later at GLOBANT and Unity Pereira, I delved deeper into game engine development, working with C++ and supporting multiple render pipelines. Currently, I am working as a Software Engineer at Microsoft, where I develop and maintain key features for the Outlook Calendar.

My technical skills include proficiency in English and Spanish, expertise in C++, C#, JavaScript, expertise in Unity Engine and understanding of rendering APIs such as DirectX, Metal, Vulkan, and OpenGL.

I'm currently pursuing Systems Engineering at Universidad Areandina. When I'm not coding, I'm likely engaging with the gaming community or exploring the latest industry trends.
